Description

The RefSeq Gene Annotations track shows the $organism version $refSeqAnnoVersion gene annotations produce by the NCBI RefSeq group.

Display Conventions and Configuration

This track follows the display conventions for gene prediction tracks.

Methods

The RefSeq Gene Predictions are produced by NCBI using the Gnomon program.

Credits

This track was produced by the NCBI RefSeq project.

References

Pruitt KD, Tatusova T, Maglott DR. NCBI Reference Sequence (RefSeq): a curated non-redundant sequence database of genomes, transcripts and proteins. Nucleic Acids Res. 2005 Jan 1;33(Database issue):D501-4.
